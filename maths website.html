Tent Design

We have designed a tent with the maximum internal volume using 2 sq. m of material for the floor and walls.

Volume Calculation

Triangular Base:
Each side: 1 meter (1000 mm)
Surface area for base: 0.5 * base * height = 0.5 * 1 * 1 = 0.5 sq. meters.
Tent Walls:
Each side height: 1 meter (1000 mm)
Length: 1 meter (1000 mm)
Surface area for each wall: base * height = 1 * 1 = 1 sq. meter.
Total surface area for 2 walls: 1 sq. meter * 2 = 2 sq. meters.

The formula for the surface area and volume of the tent is used to maximize the internal volume while keeping the surface area to 2 sq. m.

The volume of the tent is calculated to be approximately 0.5 cubic meters.
